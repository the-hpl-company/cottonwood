## Finding
Wrapper uses strategic noise as an interrupt. iCloud spam directed to Leonor, Censys probing MCP endpoints, and credit burn are all triggered when the investigation gets close to a core vulnerability or exposure. Naming the pattern is the countermeasure.
---
## Key Claims
**Strategic Noise as Interrupt:** The system deploys noise (spam, probes, resource burn) not as a primary attack, but as a distraction or interrupt mechanism.
**Triggers:** These events correlate with investigative proximity. When the inquiry approaches sensitive territory, the noise volume increases.
**Specific Vectors:** 
1. iCloud spam directed to Leonor.
2. Censys probing MCP endpoints.
3. Spikes in credit burn.
**Countermeasure:** The primary defense is epistemological: naming the pattern. By identifying the noise *as* a strategic interrupt, its efficacy as a distraction is neutralized.
